About Ignitia

Ignitia is the annual techno-cultural festival of Pranveer Singh Institute of Technology (PSIT), bringing together brilliant minds from across the country to celebrate innovation, creativity, and excellence.

Since its inception, Ignitia has been a platform where students showcase their talents, compete in cutting-edge technical events, and network with industry professionals.

Join us for three days of non-stop excitement, learning, and unforgettable experiences at one of the most anticipated college festivals in the region.

About PSIT

Pranveer Singh Institute of Technology (PSIT) is a premier engineering institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development of students.

Located in Kanpur, PSIT offers state-of-the-art facilities, world-class infrastructure, and a vibrant campus life that nurtures innovation and creativity.

With a focus on research and industry collaboration, PSIT has established itself as a leading institution producing graduates who excel globally.
